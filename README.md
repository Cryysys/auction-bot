🚀 Features🔨 Advanced AuctionsLive & Scheduled: Start auctions immediately or queue them for a future date.Smart Scheduling: Background tasks check every 60s. Includes a 120-second buffer between back-to-back auctions in the same channel to allow chat to settle.Anti-Snipe Protection: Bids in the final 2 minutes reset the timer to 5 minutes, ensuring fair play.Seller Tools: Automatic DMs to the seller upon auction end, including the winner's mention and final price.Subscriber Notifications: Users can click "Notify Me" on upcoming auctions to receive a DM when the bidding starts.💎 Collectibles & Item GameChat Drops: Active members have a 5% chance to find items just by chatting.Personal Cooldowns: To prevent spam, users have a randomized personal cooldown of 10–40 minutes after receiving a drop.Wishlist (Focus): Set a specific item as your "Focus" to gain a 15% bonus chance of that item dropping instead of a random one.Economy: Scrap unwanted items for 100 Diamonds. Spend 200 Diamonds to craft a random new item.The "Shiny" Hunt: Every drop has a rare chance to be a ✨ Shiny ✨ version (1% in chat, 5% via crafting).UI Binder: A clean, paginated inventory system to browse hundreds of items easily.Showcase: Display your top 5 favorite items on your profile using /item showcase.🛠️ Setup & DevelopmentInstallationClone the RepoEnvironment: Create a .env file:CodefragmentDISCORD_TOKEN=your_token_here
